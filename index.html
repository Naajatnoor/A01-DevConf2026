FAQ

Learn everything you need to know before joining
our developer conference.

Who can attend the conference?

Anyone interested in technology, programming, or software development is welcome. Whether you're a student, beginner, or experienced developer, this conference is for you.

Is the conference free?

The event offers both free and premium tickets. Premium passes provide access to workshops, networking sessions, and exclusive content.

What topics will be covered?

Sessions cover AI, Web Development, Cloud Computing, DevOps, Cybersecurity, Mobile Development, Open Source, and Career Growth.

Will there be hands-on workshops?

Yes. Interactive workshops led by industry experts allow attendees to build real-world projects and gain practical experience.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. Participants who attend eligible sessions and workshops will receive a digital certificate after the conference.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes.Recorded sessions will be available to registered attendees after the event for a limited time.
